Summary

Venom 3D (2018) is a superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Venom. The story follows Eddie Brock, a journalist who becomes host to an alien symbiote, which gives him extraordinary powers and a violent, monstrous alter-ego, Venom. As Eddie struggles to control the symbiote's impulses, he faces off against Carlton Drake, the head of a biotech company conducting experiments on symbiotes, who becomes the villainous Riot. Eddie and Venom must work together to stop Riot and save the world from chaos. The film mixes action, dark humor, and antihero themes, highlighting Eddie's complex relationship with the symbiote.
