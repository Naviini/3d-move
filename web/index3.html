Summary

Saw 3D, also known as Saw: The Final Chapter, is the seventh installment in the Saw horror franchise. The film follows the aftermath of Jigsaw's legacy, with a man named Bobby Dagen, who claims to have survived a Jigsaw trap, becoming a media sensation. However, his past connection to the traps is revealed, and he becomes the target of a new game. Meanwhile, Detective Hoffman continues his killing spree, trying to tie up loose ends. The film concludes the saga with a final twist, revealing key connections and bringing the long-running series to its ultimate conclusion.
