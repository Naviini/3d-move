THE HOLE

Directors
Joe Dante

Summary

The Hole 3D (2009) is a supernatural horror film directed by Joe Dante. The story follows two brothers, Dane and Lucas, who move into a new house with their mother. In the basement, they discover a mysterious locked hole in the floor. After opening it, they unleash terrifying creatures and dark forces tied to their own fears and past traumas. As the hole begins to affect their lives, the siblings must confront their worst nightmares to survive. The film combines psychological horror with supernatural elements, using 3D effects to intensify the suspense and terror.
