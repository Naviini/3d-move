WALKING WITH DINOSAURS

Directors
Tim Haines Jasper James

Summary

Walking with Dinosaurs is a 1999 BBC documentary series that uses CGI and animatronics to portray the lives of dinosaurs during the Mesozoic Era. It features their behavior, habitats, and struggles for survival, narrated like a nature documentary. Each episode focuses on different periods, showcasing various species and their interactions.
